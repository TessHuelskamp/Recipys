Raspberry Lime Mojito
=====================

Serves 1.

Ingredients
-----------

* Crushed ice
* 1 oz. simple syrup
* 2 oz. white rum
* 3 thin lime wedges
* 5 fresh mint leaves
* 5 raspberries
* Limeade

Instructions
------------

#. Place raspberries, mint and lime in a bowl. Squish.
#. Place squished mixture into tumbler. Add ice, rum, suryp, limeade.

   * should be 1 part drink mixture, 1 part limeade (approx).

#. Shake. (Vigourously.)
#. Serve.
