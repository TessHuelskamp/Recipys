Spice Cake
==========

from Jenny Wilson

Ingredients
-----------

* 1 1/2 cup sugar
* 1/2 cup butter
* 2 eggs
* 1 tsp cinnamon
* 1 tsp cloves
* 1 tsp baking soda
* 2 cups flour
* 1 cup sour Cream

Instructions
------------

#. Cream sugar and butter (melted) together
#. Add eggs, beating well
#. Add spices *alternately* with sour cream.
#. Bake at 350 for 25 min
